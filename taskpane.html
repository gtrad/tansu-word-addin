Welcome to Tansu

Variable Tracker for Word

Tansu helps you manage and insert variables into your Word documents. Keep values like prices, dates, and measurements in sync across all your files.

How it works

  1. The Tansu desktop app stores your variables locally
  2. This add-in connects to the app to display and insert them
  3. Variables stay in sync - update once, update everywhere

Why a desktop app?

Your data stays on your computer - no cloud accounts, no subscriptions, no privacy concerns. The desktop app handles Excel integration and keeps everything synced.
